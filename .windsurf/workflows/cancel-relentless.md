# /cancel-relentless — Cancel Relentless Mode

**What:** Stops relentless mode only. Equivalent to `/stop-continuation --relentless`.

**Usage:** `/cancel-relentless` or `/stop-continuation --relentless`

**Safety:** Does NOT discard changes, close session, or delete tasks. Preserves auto-continue for other todos.
